"Trailing Glory, the Creatures Move"

Wes Civilz

   

An itsy bitsy spider

rappelled down from the ceiling

right after the orgasm and

out the window we see:

 

Black cat zips through yard dodging cactus perfectly,

 

quick sweet path shimmers behind him
