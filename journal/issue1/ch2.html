"The Creeley Dreams"

Carla Harryman

   

1. Conveyance

 

Then it wasn't  

a young woman

assisting with a car trip

was it? to Pacifica Radio and

 

I will strip

poems from a book

with his name on the cover

 

It's a hefty

road

to the station. The trail

ends above

freeways

 

We need streets

but, listen

the phones

loved in houses

none knew

 

 

2. Print

 

A note with

t, o, i, e

and l for lite

toil to

toilette

 let it be

 

a hoot

and a baby toy

 a skein (after Creeley), a

wound of tiny paradise

 

a winding

pastoral

 

returns to a

canvas, through

which elephants

appeared, tilting people

forward
